Alpha port of ZFS to Mac OS

This project is an experimental alpha port of the latest ZFS subsystem, to Mac OS and Darwin.

Authors and Contributors

The search began, to recruit additional developers to find a fresh start for MacZFS. (@smuckola) (aka dtm in IRC) went out into the great wide open of IRC to solicit cross-platform collaboration, and collided with (@ryao), (@lundman), and (@wca). (@ryao) started investigating the concept of cross-development with the ZFSonLinux community, starting with the idea of the build system. Instead, (@lundman) ended up joining in with a relentless torrent of spontaneous code. He forked the github repositories of the Solaris Portability Layer (SPL) and of ZFSonLinux, and began experimenting with porting them.
